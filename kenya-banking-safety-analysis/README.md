# Kenya Banking Safety Analysis

**Published article:** [The Safest Bank in Kenya Is One You've Never Heard Of](https://medium.com/@lucylight7/the-safest-bank-in-kenya-is-one-youve-never-heard-of-0340ceb03066)

## Overview
A quantitative safety ranking of Kenya's 39 licensed commercial banks using CBK 2024 supervisory data. Banks are scored across three dimensions: Capital Adequacy, NPL ratio, and Return on Assets.

## Data Source
Central Bank of Kenya — 2024 Banking Sector Report

## Methodology
- Capital Adequacy: 75% weight
- NPL Ratio: 15% weight  
- ROA: 10% weight
- Min-max normalisation into a composite 0–1 score
- Tier thresholds: mean ± 0.5 standard deviation
