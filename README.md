## About This Portfolio

While most financial analysis focuses on Western markets, critical stories about African economies often go untold. I write to change that — diving into Kenya's banking landscape, fintech trends, and economic patterns using real data. Every article I publish on Medium is paired with complete transparency here: the raw datasets, the SQL queries I ran, and the analysis that shaped my conclusions. Think of this as your invitation to audit my work, challenge my findings, or take the analysis in a new direction. Reproducibility isn't just nice to have — it's the foundation of credible financial writing. This repo exists so readers can move from "that's interesting" to "let me verify that myself" in seconds.
